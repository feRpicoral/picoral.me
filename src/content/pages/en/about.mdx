## How I work

- **Ship the small thing.** I'd rather merge a working 60% version today than a perfect plan that ships next quarter. Production beats design doc.
- **Read the source.** When something behaves weirdly, the canonical answer is in the code, not in a blog post.
- **Boring infra under exciting features.** Tenancy, observability, error boundaries — the unglamorous layer is where products live or die.
- **Instrument before optimizing.** Numbers beat intuition. Always.
