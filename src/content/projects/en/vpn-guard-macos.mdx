A small macOS utility for verifying VPN routing. Three independent layers — /etc/hosts entries, /etc/resolver DNS shaping, and a pf packet-filter rule — that all need to pass before the configured domains resolve. A LaunchDaemon runs the verification periodically and fails safe: if any check breaks, the domains stop resolving rather than leaking.

Wrote it because the off-the-shelf solutions either depended on a single layer (defeated by a misconfigured resolver) or were Windows-only.
