Stockvio automated the monthly DARF (capital-gains tax slip) for Brazilian individual investors trading on B3 (BOVESPA). Parsed trade notes from brokers, computed the right number, and saved users a spreadsheet weekend every month.

I founded it in November 2020 and led it as CEO until February 2022. Built the trade-receipt parser in Node.js, most of the frontend in Next.js, and several backend endpoints in NestJS.
